Title: The cruel games of a cyber-bullying cartel

Content: A cruel game that plays on online anonymity and looks something like this: Cyberbullies home in on victims through social media channels and begin goading them into online emotional outbursts. And clock up each one of these outbursts as a triumph. The group profiled in the film has a hierarchical structure and calls itself the NWO, short for "New World Order" or "Never again online". The NWO members meet on the messaging platform Discord to coordinate tasks and boast about their actions online. Some hack into accounts to get personal data and intimate information. Others order pizzas or packages in the name of the victims.
